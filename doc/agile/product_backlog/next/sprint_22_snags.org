* What

A running list of single, genuinely-standalone leftover tasks from
sprint 22 stories that were re-triaged at sprint close and found to
be morally complete except for one real remaining item. Rather than
carry the whole parent story forward (or re-open it next sprint),
just the leftover task is listed here — pull it into whichever
future sprint has room, or fold it into a more natural home if one
emerges (e.g. once a related entity gets commissioned).

** Write purpose_type user manual chapter

From [[id:DCB5D77C-3BDA-40C1-A74A-F11F9A8C7FEC][Commission: purpose_type]] (4/5 tasks done this sprint; the story
itself is closed DONE). =purpose_type= is a lookup classifying
Portfolio purpose, and Portfolio itself ([[id:FD09AD84-DB59-4F22-A31F-3CB32351EE44][Commission: portfolio]]) hasn't
been commissioned yet — so there's no natural parent chapter to fold
this into the way book_status folds into Book's chapter. Write it as
its own small chapter (or a subsection once Portfolio's chapter
exists) covering the Qt UI (list, detail, history — all already
shipped and verified this sprint) and the standard values.

* Why

Sprint 22's health review found the sprint's own paperwork
untrustworthy — stories carried wholesale to the backlog inbox even
when only one task genuinely remained. This capture is the
lightweight alternative: don't re-open a story for one task, don't
silently drop the task either.
