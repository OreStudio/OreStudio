* Steps

Log in as =tenant_admin@barclays_plc= on both clients before starting.
Open Purpose Types via Trading > Trading Codes > Purpose Types.

** blue

*** List window loads

Open Purpose Types; confirm the MDI list window loads and displays the
existing purpose_type records (Code, Name, Description, Display Order,
Version, Modified By, Recorded At columns).

*** Detail dialog edits and persists

Double-click a record to open its detail dialog; change the
description, save; reopen the record and confirm the change persisted.

*** History dialog shows the edit

Right-click the edited record and open its history; confirm the prior
version appears, and the History dialog's Close button actually closes
the window (regression check — this was broken and fixed during
commissioning).

*** Delete preserves history

Create a throwaway purpose type, delete it from the list, then confirm
it disappears from the active list but its history is still reachable
(e.g. via a version/revert dialog if the UI exposes one, or by
confirming the delete doesn't hard-remove the row).

** green

*** Confirm the cross-client eventing notification arrives

With =blue='s edit from the previous step already saved, confirm
=green='s Purpose Types list updated live (recency highlight, no
manual refresh) shortly after the save. Then make a fresh edit in
=green= itself and confirm it appears live in =blue=.
