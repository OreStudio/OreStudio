* Decisions

Abandoned 2026-07-10 at sprint 22 close: the premise (book/portfolio
belong in =ores.trading=, not =ores.refdata=) doesn't hold up against
how established trading systems draw this line. Murex's *Static Data*
module and Calypso's *Reference Data domain* both explicitly place
books and portfolios alongside counterparties, legal entities, and
currencies — not alongside trades, positions, or cashflows — because
the classifying test is lifecycle/mutation pattern (created rarely by
back-office setup, referenced constantly by trades) rather than
topical proximity to trading. This also matches how this sprint's
work on book (=commission_book=, =commission_book_status=,
=book_data_model_cleanup=) already treated it as a refdata entity
throughout, with no friction. Full reasoning recorded in [[id:130A3261-E788-4222-9BD7-371AD92982FB][Book]]§"Where
this lives in ORE Studio". Not recaptured under a different direction
— =ores.refdata= is already correct, nothing needs to move.
