This page is a [[id:671F18E4-E09C-4B3B-BD24-D33DF8AE38A6][capture]] in the *inbox* bucket of the product backlog — a pre-sprint idea, not yet pulled into a sprint as a story.

* What

Write a user-manual chapter documenting =business_unit= and
=business_unit_type= as reference-data entities in their own right, covering
the org-structure concept, the Business Units and Business Unit Types list
windows, and their detail dialogs. Both entities have full Qt implementations
under =ores.qt.refdata=
([[file:../../../../../projects/ores.qt/refdata/src/BusinessUnitMdiWindow.cpp][BusinessUnitMdiWindow]],
[[file:../../../../../projects/ores.qt/refdata/src/BusinessUnitDetailDialog.cpp][BusinessUnitDetailDialog]],
[[file:../../../../../projects/ores.qt/refdata/src/BusinessUnitTypeMdiWindow.cpp][BusinessUnitTypeMdiWindow]],
[[file:../../../../../projects/ores.qt/refdata/src/BusinessUnitTypeDetailDialog.cpp][BusinessUnitTypeDetailDialog]])
but neither appears anywhere in the manual.

* Why

Discovered while auditing the party/counterparty/party_status commissioning
story for manual coverage of auxiliary lookup entities: business unit is a
distinct org-structure concept (not a party/counterparty combo source like
party_status/party_id_scheme are), and warrants its own chapter rather than a
slot inside the Parties chapter.
