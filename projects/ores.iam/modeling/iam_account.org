An Account is the fundamental digital identity and record within the [[id:D00BEA0D-E501-C534-A013-E6F40C1A6097][IAM system]]
for a distinct entity that interacts with the platform. It serves as the primary
container for authentication credentials, authorization attributes, and
descriptive metadata.

An account uniquely binds an entity (/e.g./, a human user, a software service, a
system component) to a persistent profile that governs its access and
capabilities.
