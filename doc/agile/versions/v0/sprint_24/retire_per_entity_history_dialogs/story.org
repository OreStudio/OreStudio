This page documents a [[id:699A8D45-B67E-4D3E-9206-24FA17C51ADA][story]] in [[id:D269862E-2035-4857-A0D0-A6E93B0E24C9][Sprint 24]]. It captures the goal, current status, acceptance criteria, and the tasks that compose it.

* Goal

Split from Sprint 23's [[id:037B474D-84ED-4888-9054-D58AB1FB10D2][Consolidate history dialogs onto HistoryDialogBase]]
at close: that story shipped the entire new history architecture —
=ores.diff= with intra-value diff spans, one generic
=history.v1.get= NATS subject dispatched server-side to codegen'd
per-entity field mappers, a single non-templated =HistoryDialog=
widget shared by every entity (replacing the =HistoryDialogBase=/
per-entity-subclass design entirely), a generic unified-diff shell
renderer, and the Gemini-review UI redesign. =ores.qt.refdata= has
already fully migrated onto it.

What's left is the actual cleanup: once every remaining =ores.qt=
component (admin, analytics, compute, marketdata, synthetic,
trading, workspace) has migrated its entities onto the generic
=HistoryDialog= — each tracked separately as a "Commission
ores.qt.<component> into codegen" backlog capture — delete
=HistoryDialogBase=, every remaining per-entity =*HistoryDialog=
class, and their templates.
