This page documents a [[id:31C868B9-306E-4282-BA8C-07E647021B34][task]] in the [[id:E5EC80BB-789B-4BD6-B6E5-DC7AA790692A][Synthetic data librarian support: FX foundation]] story. It captures the goal, current status, acceptance, and any notes or results.

* Goal

Produce a concrete design for a new =ores.codegen= facet
(provisionally =publish_from_dq=) that, from a single declaration on an
entity's existing codegen model, generates the mechanical parts of the
publish-from-dq pattern documented in
[[id:F05B779F-769B-49D5-B369-D2AAC029B36D][DQ Bundle Publication Pipeline]]'s codegen-value-assessment table:
the artefact-type registration row, a shadow-table model *derived*
from the entity model (removing today's hand-duplicated
=lookup_entity= file), the =SECURITY DEFINER=
=ores_<service>_publish_<entity>_from_dq_fn= SQL function, the NATS
=publish_from_dq_handler=, and its registrar wiring. Use the
=report_definitions=/=ore_analytics= worked example in that doc as the
concrete shape to generalise from (mode semantics, natural-key upsert,
naming conventions).

* Acceptance

- Design covers all five generated artefacts (artefact-type row,
  derived shadow-table model, publish function, handler, registrar
  wiring) with a clear input declaration on the entity model.
- Design explicitly states how =mode= (upsert/insert_only/replace_all)
  and natural-key matching are parameterised, since these vary slightly
  across the 25 existing hand-written functions.
- Design is validated against at least two existing hand-written
  instances (e.g. =report_definitions= and one =refdata= entity) to
  confirm it can reproduce their behaviour.
