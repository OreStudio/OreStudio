* What

The agile-board timeline chart renders the entire period in a single view.
At 20-minute granularity over many hours (see the Sprint 20 backfill) the
chart is too crammed to read. Add a *moving time-window* — show a fixed span
(e.g. a few hours, or N buckets) at a time — with *back/forward* controls (and
ideally zoom) to pan through time, so any window stays scannable. Surfaced
while reviewing the regenerated Sprint 20 timeline.

* Why

The whole-period chart becomes unreadable as soon as activity spans more than
a couple of hours; a windowed, navigable view is the difference between a
usable timeline and a wall of bars.
