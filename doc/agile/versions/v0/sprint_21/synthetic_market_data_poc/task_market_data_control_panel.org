* Goal

Provide an operator control panel for market data feeds that does NOT need to know the feed's source (synthetic, vendor, ...). It lists known feeds with running/stopped/health state and offers start/stop/monitor actions. For the PoC it drives the synthetic FX spot feed via marketdata.v1.market_feed_configs.start/.stop, but the panel itself is source-agnostic: a feed is identified by its source name / config, and the start/stop is dispatched to whichever backend owns it. Designed to grow to other market-data types (vol surfaces, IR) and other sources (real vendor feeds) without UI rework. Lives at the market-data level (e.g. ores.qt.mktdata, Market Data menu), separate from the synthetic config authoring UI.

* Acceptance

- A Market Data menu entry opens a control panel listing feeds with state
  (running/stopped/health).
- Start/Stop actions control a feed; for the PoC this drives the synthetic FX
  spot feed and observations/ticks appear (or stop) in the chart.
- The panel models a feed source-agnostically (by source name/config), not as
  "the synthetic feed"; no synthetic-specific assumptions in the panel.
- No raw NATS request needed; issued authenticated via the client.
- Decision recorded: the source-agnostic feed/control abstraction (how
  start/stop dispatches to the owning backend).
