* Goal

Give sprint 23 a real starting backlog: work through the product
backlog and past-sprint carry-overs, promoting well-specified
captures into sprint 23 stories and pulling in stories that were
misfiled (wrong bucket/sprint folder) or mislabeled (marked ABANDONED
in a sprint table with no corresponding decision recorded), verifying
each one's premise against the live codebase rather than trusting its
own text. Also extend the =agile-refine-backlog= skill with a deep
mode, so future backlog audits of this kind are a repeatable,
reported procedure rather than one-off manual work.

* Acceptance

- Sprint 23 has a working set of well-specified, verified stories to
  pick up (not just "Open sprint 23" scaffolding).
- Every misfiled/mislabeled sprint-22/sprint-19 story found is
  corrected: moved to its correct sprint folder, filetags/parent-links
  fixed, and wired into the correct sprint's Stories table.
- Every promoted capture is verified against the current codebase
  before being trusted (confirmed still-unaddressed, not already
  resolved elsewhere).
- Duplicate captures are merged with no content loss, not silently
  deleted.
- =agile-refine-backlog= gains a deep mode covering inbox/next/deferred
  with a written report, so this kind of audit doesn't require
  re-deriving the process each time.
