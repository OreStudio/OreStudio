* Goal

Today the EUR/USD market_series and its observations are stamped with the system tenant (the synthetic service account's tenant), set at startup in application.cpp where no business tenant exists. Consequently a business-tenant login (e.g. Barclays) sees an empty Market Series list; only system-tenant principals (super_admin) see the data. Make the target tenant a property of the feed configuration: the start request (and persisted named config) carries the tenant; the series bootstrap moves from service startup to feed-start time under that tenant; observations are stamped with it. This likely needs the synthetic service to act on behalf of the target tenant (delegated auth) rather than as a system service writing cross-tenant. Coordinate with the named-sources and feed-config-UI tasks.

* Acceptance

- The target tenant is supplied as feed configuration (start request /
  persisted named config), not the synthetic service's own tenant.
- The EUR/USD series and its observations are created under that business
  tenant; the series bootstrap happens at feed-start, not service startup.
- A business-tenant login sees the series/observations in Market Series and the
  chart; the system tenant is no longer used for synthetic market data.
- Writes are authorised as the target tenant (delegation or equivalent), not a
  cross-tenant system write.
- Decision recorded: how the synthetic service obtains tenant-scoped authority
  for the write.
