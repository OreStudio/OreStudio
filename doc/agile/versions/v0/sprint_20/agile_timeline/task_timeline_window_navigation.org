* Goal

The board timeline chart shows a sliding window of buckets with back/forward
navigation, instead of rendering every bucket at once (which crammed the bars
and made the x-axis labels overlap into an unreadable smear).

* Acceptance

- The timeline chart shows a fixed window of buckets (default 10), not the
  whole timeline.
- ← earlier / later → buttons page the window by a windowful, clamped at the
  ends, with a range indicator (=from … to · N–M of T=).
- Clicking a bar still opens that bucket's snapshot (selection maps to the
  absolute bucket index across windows).
- The default window is the most recent buckets.
