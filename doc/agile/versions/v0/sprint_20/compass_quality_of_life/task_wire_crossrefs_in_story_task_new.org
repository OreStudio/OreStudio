* Goal

The Goto pillar (=compass story new= / =compass task new=) wires its
cross-references and is born with content, matching the Scaffold pillar
(=compass add=).

Observed gap (2026-06-08, while scaffolding the =suggest_next_work_item=
story and the =reuse_built_site_in_deploy= task):

- =compass add task= *does* wire the new row into the parent story's
  =* Tasks= table (prints "🔗 Wired into … (* Tasks, …)").
- =compass story new= and =compass task new= do *not*: the new task is left
  out of the story's =* Tasks= table, and =story new= leaves the new story
  out of the sprint's =* Stories= table — both are printed only as manual
  "next steps".
- None of =story new= / =task new= accept =--goal= / =--acceptance=, so the
  docs are born with placeholder Goal/Acceptance and must be filled by hand.

The inconsistency is the bug: the Goto pillar builds on the Scaffold pillar
but skips the wiring the Scaffold pillar already performs.
