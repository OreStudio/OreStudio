The authority half of the [[id:DF2B616F-E112-4C57-A73B-69F85BE96EF4][Market Data Architecture]].

* Goal

Make =ores.marketdata= the authority: a source-agnostic *feed-config binding*
records which producer channel feeds each official series; an ingest loop
subscribes to the bound producer channels, *persists* observations (tenant +
source) and *remaps* them to the official tenant-scoped stream consumers use.

* Acceptance

- A *feed-config (binding)* entity in marketdata: official series ← producer +
  source channel + producer-config reference + =enabled=/=auto=; CRUD + NATS
  protocol; source-agnostic. (M1)
- An *ingest loop* subscribes to the bound producer channels, persists each
  observation (tenant + source) as the sole writer, and *remaps/re-publishes* to
  the official stream. (M2)
- The official stream is *tenant-scoped* (=marketdata.v1.tick.<tenant>.<key>=);
  consumers (=fx_spot_subscription=, chart) subscribe to it. (M3)
- A *CRM derivation seam* exists — marketdata can compute derived rates from
  drivers (trivial single-pair now; pluggable for the full [[id:B38B3869-02FD-4CC7-99BD-9A77904ACA19][CRM]] later). (M4)
- Switching a series' source is a feed-config edit with no consumer change.
