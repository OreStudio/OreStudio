This page is a [[id:671F18E4-E09C-4B3B-BD24-D33DF8AE38A6][capture]] in the *inbox* bucket of the product backlog — a pre-sprint idea, not yet pulled into a sprint as a story.

* What

Take an inventory of every entity exposed by ores.qt.admin: which have a codegen model, which are still hand-rolled, and which have a hand-rolled *HistoryDialog. For entities with no model yet, author one. Then run full-stack codegen for every entity in the component and reconcile any drift, so each entity's history view lands on the generic HistoryDialog widget rather than a per-entity hand-rolled dialog.

* Why

The "Migrate ores.qt.admin's history dialogs to the generic HistoryDialog" task originally lived under the [[id:DBDFF0B1-E358-4300-B361-644A89FBD169][Migrate ores.qt.refdata onto the generic HistoryDialog]] story, but that story is being narrowed to ores.qt.refdata only. ores.qt.admin still needs its entities inventoried, commissioned through codegen, and migrated onto the generic HistoryDialog — this capture tracks that as its own piece of work, sized and scheduled independently.
