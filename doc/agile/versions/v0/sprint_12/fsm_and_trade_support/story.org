* Decisions

- /FSM lives in Postgres/ :: trade-lifecycle authority is the database.
- /Trade envelope, not trade record/ :: holds identity + temporal versioning + party roles.
- /Rename component/ :: =ores.trading= is more precise than =ores.trade=.

* Out of scope

- Trade computation (lives in =ores.ore= / =ores.compute=).
