* Goal

Extend =ores.synthetic='s =organisation_generator_service= (not a
from-scratch script) to produce Acme Bank's party hierarchy, LEIs,
contacts, and staff accounts against the hand-authored company
structure from the analysis task, plus its business
units/portfolios/books — the latter now unblocked by [[https://github.com/OreStudio/OreStudio/pull/1662][PR #1662]]
(prime_origin's data-driven bundle-publish refactor, merged
2026-07-22): populate Acme's org/portfolio/book structure through the
new =risk_management= bundle rather than a bespoke path. See
[[proj:projects/ores.seeder/datasets/acme_bank_structure_analysis/dataset_overview.org][acme_bank_structure_analysis]] for the full proposed shape (branches,
desks, headcounts, LEI scheme) and the =ores.synthetic= gaps
(fixed-shape vs. random tree, no staff layer, no HK locale, LEI not
checksum-valid) this task closes.
