Users should be able to enter expiry dates as a shorthand (e.g. =9Y=, =6M=, =1W=) rather than a full calendar date. This requires:

1. A calendar service or integration (QuantLib =Calendar= or equivalent).
2. A settlement rule (which business day convention, which holiday calendar).
3. UI: a field that accepts either a date or a shorthand string; the resolved date is shown alongside.

This depends on calendar infrastructure that is not yet in place. Backlog until that work is scoped.
