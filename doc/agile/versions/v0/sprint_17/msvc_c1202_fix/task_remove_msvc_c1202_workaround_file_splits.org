** Summary

Removes three sets of artificial code splits that were introduced solely to work around MSVC C1202 (constexpr budget exceeded by rfl template depth). The root fix — =#pragma constexpr_steps(1000000)= / =#pragma constexpr_depth(1024)= in the affected TU — was applied in PR #786.

- *=[qt]=* Merge =ClientManagerSetup.cpp= and =ClientManagerEvents.cpp= back into =ClientManager.cpp=. The four methods (=testConnection=, =signup=, =subscribeToEvent=, =unsubscribeFromEvent=) were split out purely to reduce per-TU rfl instantiation depth. Adds a single MSVC pragma block at the top of =ClientManager.cpp= and deletes the two workaround files.

- *=[trading]=* Replace =register_other_instrument_handlers= (a catch-all for 6 instrument families) with six dedicated per-family registrars matching the structure already used for FX and rates: =register_bond_handlers=, =register_credit_handlers=, =register_equity_handlers=, =register_commodity_handlers=, =register_composite_handlers=, =register_scripted_handlers=. Also corrects the stale =subs.reserve(113)= → 105 and consolidates the insert boilerplate in =registrar.cpp=.
