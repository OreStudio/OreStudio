* Prerequisites

- Services running: =compass services start=.
- A fresh, Barclays-provisioned database with the ORE reference vintage imported:
  #+begin_src sh :results verbatim
  compass db recreate -y -k
  compass services start
  compass shell -f projects/ores.shell/scripts/library/provisioning/barclays_system_provision.ores
  compass shell -u tenant_admin@barclays_plc -p Secure-Password-123 \
    -f projects/ores.shell/scripts/library/marketdata/import_legacy_example_56.ores
  #+end_src
- A running Qt client (not yet logged in — see first step below). The
  Barclays party ships two pre-seeded FX rates: =synthetic.eurusd= and
  =synthetic.gbpusd=, both =price_source = vintage=,
  =vintage_source = ore.reference=, =vintage_date = 2016-02-05=.

* Steps

** Log in

Username: =tenant_admin@barclays_plc=. Password: =Secure-Password-123=.
Confirm the =BARCLAYS PLC= party is active after login.

** Open Market Simulator

Menu: *Market Data > Synthetic > Market Simulator*. Confirm the two
pre-seeded FX rates (=synthetic.eurusd=, =synthetic.gbpusd=) are
listed.

** Vintage-derived feed starts at the real imported spot

Select =synthetic.eurusd=, click *Start*. Expected: the feed starts
successfully (no rejection), and the tick chart's initial price is
=1.132337= — the real imported EUR/USD spot, not a placeholder. Click
*Stop* afterwards.

** Guard rejects a missing vintage

Edit =synthetic.eurusd=. Confirm *From vintage data* is selected.
Change *Vintage date* to =2099-01-01= (no imported data for that date)
and *Save*. Click *Start*. Expected: rejected with "No vintage data
found for source=ore.reference, date=2099-01-01." — no feed starts.
Edit the rate again and restore *Vintage date* to =2016-02-05=; *Save*.

** Fixed spot mode starts at the entered price

Edit =synthetic.gbpusd=. Switch the radio to *Fixed*. Confirm *Initial
price* becomes enabled and *Vintage source*/*Vintage date* become
disabled. Set *Initial price* to =1.25= and *Save*. Click *Start*.
Expected: the feed starts immediately (no vintage lookup performed) at
=1.25=. Click *Stop*, edit the rate again, switch back to *From vintage
data* (re-enter =ore.reference= / =2016-02-05=), and *Save* to restore
the original seeded state.

** Half-filled price source is rejected client-side

Edit any rate, select *From vintage data*, but leave *Vintage source*
or *Vintage date* blank, then click *Save*. Expected: a warning dialog
— "Vintage source and date are both required when the price source is
'From vintage data'." — no save request sent.

** "Validate Vintage" toolbar action reports per-rate status

With at least one vintage-mode and one fixed-mode rate configured,
click the *Validate Vintage* toolbar button (checkmark icon). Expected
summary dialog: vintage-mode rates with real data show "✓ <label> —
Vintage data available (<price>)"; fixed-mode rates show "✓ <label> —
fixed spot — no vintage to check"; a vintage-mode rate with a missing
vintage shows "✗ <label> — No vintage data found for source=...,
date=...". No feeds are started by this action.
