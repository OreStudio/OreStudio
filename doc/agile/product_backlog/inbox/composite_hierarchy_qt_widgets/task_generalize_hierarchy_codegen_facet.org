This page documents a [[id:31C868B9-306E-4282-BA8C-07E647021B34][task]] in the [[id:131C2E23-F41F-4F7A-B9EC-378710AF055B][Composite child-entity and hierarchy Qt widgets for codegen]] story. It captures the goal, current status, acceptance, and any notes or results.

* Goal

Give party and counterparty a single mechanism to fetch their whole
self-referencing hierarchy in one server-side round trip, instead of
the naive alternative of fetching a paginated flat entity list
client-side and merging pages (which fragments the tree at page
boundaries if a child's parent lands on a different page).

Scope narrowed during implementation, by explicit decision:
- =has_parent_id= is a *Flag* (a cross-cutting property like
  =has_tenant_id=), not a "facet" — corrected terminology, see
  =org_entity_meta_model.org=.
- The flag generates *only* the recursive-CTE hierarchy query
  function, not the parent column or its insert-trigger validation —
  those stay hand-maintained (party/counterparty's existing
  validation is untouched, not migrated onto the flag).
- The NATS message/handler is hand-written for party and counterparty
  first, not codegen-generated — proving the pattern before investing
  in protocol/service/handler template work, same approach already
  used for the Qt hierarchy widgets.
- =book= (net-new hierarchy support) is out of scope here — filed as
  a follow-up capture instead of attempted in this pass.
