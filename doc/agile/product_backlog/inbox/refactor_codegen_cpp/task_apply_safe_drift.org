* Goal

Apply safe drift to the =refdata-cpp= component and use it as the *reference
implementation* for the per-component drift workflow. The remaining 11
components each get their own task that follows this pattern.

** Why refdata is the pilot

Refdata is the most heterogeneous component:
- 25 domain_entity models (more than any other component)
- Mix of UUID-PK and string-PK entities
- Mix of stateful and stateless repositories in production
- Child entities (contact_information, identifier) with by-parent lookup
- =party_repository= has hand-crafted methods (=read_system_party=, pagination,
  CTE descendants) that cannot be regenerated

Solving refdata establishes the template flags and exclusion pattern that
everything else inherits.
