ORE Studio is in early active development (version 0.x). Releases are published
on [[https://github.com/OreStudio/OreStudio/releases][GitHub]] and are provided for evaluation and learning purposes only. Many
problems are expected — do not use these builds for production, real trading, or
any financial decision-making. See the [[id:6A1A333C-A83A-8954-B9C3-C3AB1AEC3046][User Manual]] for guidance on getting
started.
