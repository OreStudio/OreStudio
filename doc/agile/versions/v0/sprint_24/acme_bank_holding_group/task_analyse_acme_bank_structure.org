* Goal

Before building anything, do an in-depth analysis to ground the
Acme Bank corporate structure and figure out what's realistically
populatable with the entities ORE Studio supports today:

- Research Barclays' actual corporate structure (holding company,
  legal entity subsidiaries, divisions such as Barclays UK, Barclays
  International/Investment Bank, business centres/locations) as the
  reference shape for Acme Bank.
- Map that structure onto ORE Studio's current entity model (party
  hierarchy, business units, portfolios, books, accounts/staff,
  roles/permissions) and identify what can be populated now versus
  what needs new entity support first.
- Produce a proposed Acme Bank structure: the party tree, which
  divisions trade derivatives (and therefore need the full
  business-unit/portfolio/book chain), the business centres/locales
  involved, and the staff roles to generate per desk.
- Set up the output as a dataset through the Data Librarian workflow,
  so the analysis is captured and trackable as first-class reference
  data rather than a one-off document.

* Result

Landed the analysis as [[proj:projects/ores.seeder/datasets/acme_bank_structure_analysis/dataset_overview.org][acme_bank_structure_analysis]], a first-class
=ores.seeder= dataset:

- Barclays' real corporate structure researched and cited (CIB
  Markets as the reference shape for the trading floor).
- Proposed Acme Bank party hierarchy: Group → International → three
  branches (London, New York, Hong Kong), each with a checksum-valid
  synthetic LEI on GLEIF's reserved =9695= pre-LOU test prefix, so
  Acme integrates with the real GLEIF counterparty universe rather
  than being an isolated fictional world (contrast with =slovaris=).
- Trading floor + Middle Office + Market Risk support functions
  proposed per branch (49 staff accounts total), with locale-matched
  names and FaceStudio-sourced profile pictures.
- Gaps identified: no locale-aware name generation, no account
  profile-picture support (accounts have no image field; the existing
  images table is SVG-only), only 1 business centre seeded today, no
  DQ artefact type for tracking generated org/staff data, and RBAC
  roles don't reach desk granularity.
- Scope decisions made with the user: existing orphaned business
  unit/portfolio/book/report fixtures should be re-parented under
  Acme; business-unit/portfolio/book *population* is deferred until
  =prime_origin='s unified DQ dataset-publish refactor lands; picture
  storage will generalise the existing =ores_assets_images_tbl=
  rather than add a parallel table.
