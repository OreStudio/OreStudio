* What

A Gemini UI/UX review of the FX Spot Monitor widget
([[id:9242195E-21AE-4332-8306-183767046BC3][full audit and mockup]]) identified four concrete issues: (1) colors the
entire currency-pair label red/green instead of reserving color for
directional price deltas only, causing "Christmas tree" visual fatigue;
(2) numeric columns aren't right-aligned, so decimal places don't stack
for fast vertical scanning; (3) renders every pair to 5 decimal places,
violating the standard JPY convention of 3 decimals; (4) repeats the
literal text "LIVE" on every row instead of a compact status indicator.

* Why

The widget is otherwise functionally correct (confirmed working
end-to-end with real ticking synthetic feeds in
[[id:73E7622C-CBD2-48C8-96B9-DEA8AA7C45C6][Seed Basic and Realistic dataset bundles]]) but hard to actually read
at a glance, which matters for a live-ticking monitor meant to be
scanned quickly.
