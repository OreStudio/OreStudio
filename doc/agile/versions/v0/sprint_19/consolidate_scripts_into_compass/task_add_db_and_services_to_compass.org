* Goal

Bring the database lifecycle and the services lifecycle under compass,
completing two pillars from the design task in one cutover:

- *Provision / db* — =compass db recreate | setup | drop | sql |
  reset-system | reset-tenant=, replacing the =projects/ores.sql/=
  scripts of the same names (=run_sql.sh= becomes =db sql=).
- *Operate / services* — =compass services start | stop | status |
  clear-logs= and =compass client=, replacing
  =build/scripts/{start,stop,status}-services.sh=, =clear-logs.sh=
  and =start-client.sh=.

Hard cutover per the story acceptance: the migrated scripts are
deleted; the [[id:BA6F2BF1-E16E-4E80-A3D0-CACC30FA04DB][developer scripts inventory]] drops their rows and the
compass docs gain them; every recipe, knowledge doc and CI reference
is repointed to the compass verbs.

Absorbs the two placeholder tasks =migrate_operate_pillar= and
=migrate_provision_db_nats= (the nats half of the latter already
shipped as =compass nats=).

Out of scope: =recreate_env.sh= / =teardown_env.sh= (whole-environment
wrappers — candidates for =compass env=), =service_vars.sh=
(generated include), and the =utility/= one-offs.
