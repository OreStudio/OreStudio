This page is a [[id:671F18E4-E09C-4B3B-BD24-D33DF8AE38A6][capture]] in the *next* bucket of the product backlog — a pre-sprint idea, not yet pulled into a sprint as a story.

* What

Move [[proj:projects/ores.codegen/scripts/regenerate_backlog_indexes.py][=projects/ores.codegen/scripts/regenerate_backlog_indexes.py=]] into
=ores.compass= and expose it as =compass backlog reindex= (or similar
subcommand). The script walks =doc/agile/product_backlog/{inbox,next,deferred}/=
and rewrites the three index =.org= files; it belongs alongside the other
=compass backlog= operations rather than in =ores.codegen= scripts.

Delete the standalone script from =ores.codegen= once compass provides the
equivalent command. Update any CMake targets or CI steps that call the script
directly.

* Why

=regenerate_backlog_indexes.py= was added to =ores.codegen= as a convenience
script, but all other backlog-related commands (=compass capture=, =compass
backlog=) live in =ores.compass=. Splitting the tooling across two projects
means a contributor must know which project owns which backlog operation.
Consolidating into compass gives a single entry point for all backlog
management and makes the operation discoverable via =compass --help=.
