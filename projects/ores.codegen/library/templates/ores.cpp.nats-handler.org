* Summary

One template: the subject-handler skeleton dispatching an entity's
protocol messages to its service. =nats-handler= profile.

* The ores.cpp.nats-handler facet

The dispatch point of the entity's NATS surface: receives
[[id:06B4D0EE-9FD7-4616-83E9-B3CDDA1C7246][ores.cpp.protocol]] messages on the entity's subjects and routes them
to [[id:FC3A81FE-54F6-4119-8369-A7B8EF423E47][ores.cpp.service]]. Wired to its subjects by
[[id:287FF186-24CC-40D3-95B2-CEA12E3B6F3F][ores.cpp.nats-sub-registrar]]; the component-side host that runs the
handler lives in [[id:A5B138BE-4244-42C5-9EE7-FF9B4C8AB6B3][ores.cpp.service-app]].
