#+title: Ledger Sweep
#+description: The daily, automated, accounting-layer FX Revaluation process that translates every ledger book's transactional-currency balances into its functional currency.
#+type: knowledge
#+level: cross
#+filetags: :domain:trading:book:finance:currency:
#+created: 2026-07-15
#+updated: 2026-07-15

* Summary

The ledger sweep is a daily, automated, *ledger-side* process,
distinct from [[id:53841E4A-C5F3-4B7A-A269-45B8010C708A][Spot Sweep]]. The ledger receives all positions — live
and settled cash — in [[id:4ADDDA46-21CE-4C94-9F22-F66805B13891][transactional currency]]. Once a day, an
automated process spot-converts these into each book's [[id:E4FC332F-C404-4408-8E10-586FAF4ECCFC][functional
currency]], capturing the resulting P&L into a dedicated *FX
Revaluation* entry. It is a [[id:CA299D08-AC97-4A37-B3F2-A5927F379326][Sweep]] in the general sense, but unlike
spot sweep it is not opt-in, applies to every ledger book
unconditionally, and produces only an unrealised valuation entry
rather than a real, executed trade.

* Detail

** What it is

The ledger receives all positions — live and settled cash — in
transactional currency. Once a day, an automated process
spot-converts these into each book's functional currency, capturing
the resulting P&L into a dedicated *FX Revaluation* entry — see [[id:0CAD6D4D-604A-4FB9-A823-5231E759D760][FX
Spot Revaluation Types]] for the three distinct accounting treatments
(Balance Sheet, Position Account, Forward Position reval) this entry
can take. This whole daily conversion is what "ledger sweep" refers
to.

#+BEGIN_EXAMPLE
  Book positions (transactional ccy) ──► daily spot conversion ──►
      Functional-currency balance + FX Revaluation P&L entry
#+END_EXAMPLE

** Unconditional, unlike spot sweep

The ledger separates realised from unrealised P&L; ledger sweep's FX
Revaluation output is unrealised, reflecting a valuation conversion
rather than an executed trade. Ledger sweep and [[id:53841E4A-C5F3-4B7A-A269-45B8010C708A][Spot Sweep]] share a
name and a conceptual goal (cleaning up cross-currency exposure) but
operate at entirely different layers, and =Is Sweepable= governs spot
sweep eligibility only — it has no bearing on ledger sweep, which
applies to every book with a functional currency regardless of that
flag. See [[id:D215AB01-8BE4-4E2B-930C-17AC80491C9D][Book and the ledger]] for the wider ledger-sweep mechanics
(functional currency, the daily automated process), and [[id:CA299D08-AC97-4A37-B3F2-A5927F379326][Sweep]] for
the full comparison table against spot sweep.
