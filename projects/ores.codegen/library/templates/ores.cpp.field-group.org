* Summary

One template: the field-group struct — a reusable column block
composed into entities (nested instrument decomposition), rendered
from its own field-group org-model. =field-group= profile.

* The ores.cpp.field-group facet

A standalone model format: a field-group org-model is a first-class
codegen input that produces exactly one header. Unlike the entity
facets there is no layered projection — one model, one artefact.
Sibling to [[id:71124286-50D3-4E3F-A948-43A67690FFB3][ores.cpp.enum]].
