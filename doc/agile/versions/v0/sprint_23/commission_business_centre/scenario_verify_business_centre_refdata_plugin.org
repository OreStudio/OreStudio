** Log in and select Barclays Plc

Log in as =tenant_admin@barclays_plc= / =Secure-Password-123=, select
=BARCLAYS PLC=. Confirm all menus enable with no errors.

** Business Centres opens from the Reference Data menu and behaves normally

Open Reference Data > Business Centres (top level, alongside
Currencies, Countries, Currency Pairs, Books). Confirm the list window
opens, shows seeded rows (e.g. USNY, GBLO), and that add/edit/save/
history all work exactly as before. Confirm Reference Data > Parties
(Organisation area) no longer has a Business Centres entry — only
Parties, Counterparties, and Business Units remain there.

** Business Centre appears on the toolbar

Confirm the Business Centres toolbar icon is present and opens the
same list window as the menu action.

** Business Unit's business-centre dropdown still works

Open Reference Data > Business Units > add/edit. Confirm the business
centre combo box still populates correctly and flag icons render —
this dropdown reads the same =business_centre= service data, just via
a controller now owned by a different plugin.

** Logout and re-login works cleanly

Log out, then log back in (same or different party). Confirm no
crash and the Business Centres menu action and window still work
after re-login.

** No error toast or log error during the whole flow

Check the client's log tail
(=build/output/<preset>/publish/log/ores.qt.<colour>.log=) for any
ERROR-level line.
