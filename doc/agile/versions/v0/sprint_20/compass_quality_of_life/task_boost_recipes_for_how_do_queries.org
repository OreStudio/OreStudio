* Goal

compass search already has a recipe-float pass for question-shaped queries (starting with 'how', 'what', etc.), but the recipe boost only applies when content words survive stopword removal and a title match is found. A query like 'how do I ssh' surfaces recipes inconsistently. Ensure that any query starting with 'how do' (case-insensitive) always floats recipes to the top of results, ahead of tasks, stories, and memories that happen to share keywords.
