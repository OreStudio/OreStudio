* What

The paper "Gaussian GenAI — Synthetic Market Data Generation" (SSRN
5050372) proposes a methodology for generating synthetic financial
market data using Gaussian generative AI models.  The approach is
designed to produce realistic price and volatility time-series that
preserve the statistical properties of real market data (fat tails,
autocorrelation, volatility clustering) without exposing proprietary
or sensitive data.  Evaluate the paper's methodology against the data
requirements of the dynamic milestone: could synthetic market data
substitute for or augment real data in scenario generation, back-testing,
and risk model calibration?

* Why

The dynamic milestone needs realistic market data (rates, spreads,
volatility surfaces) for pricing and scenario analysis.  Sourcing or
licensing real data is expensive and legally constrained.  A validated
synthetic data methodology could remove that dependency entirely, and
also enable stress-testing with extreme-but-plausible scenarios that
rarely appear in historical data.
