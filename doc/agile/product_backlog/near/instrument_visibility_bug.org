Instruments do not appear in the instruments view after the equity per-type migration. Possible causes:

- The read path for some instrument types may not yet be wired to the Qt service layer.
- The import pipeline may not trigger a refresh of the instruments list.
- RLS visibility rules may exclude the logged-in party from seeing the instruments.

Steps:
1. Confirm whether instruments are present in the database after import.
2. Trace the Qt service call for the instruments list and verify the response.
3. Fix whichever layer is missing: service wiring, RLS policy, or UI refresh.
