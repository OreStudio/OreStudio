Future work under the Market data generation epic; proves the
[[id:DF2B616F-E112-4C57-A73B-69F85BE96EF4][Market Data Architecture]] is genuinely source-agnostic.

* Goal

Add a second market data producer service implementing the tick contract (e.g.
=ores.bloomberg=), so an official series can be sourced from a vendor instead of
synthetic with no consumer or marketdata change.

* Acceptance

- A new producer (e.g. =ores.bloomberg=) publishes ticks on its own namespace
  (=bloomberg.v1.tick.<key>=) implementing the marketdata tick contract.
- A feed-config can bind an official series to the new producer with no consumer
  or marketdata code change.
- The control panel manages it without producer-specific code.
