* Prerequisites

- Services running: =compass services start=.
- A logged-in Qt client, with at least one dataset that has several
  upstream/downstream dependencies (so the lineage graph is dense
  enough to be worth zooming/panning, and so it doesn't already fit
  entirely inside the view at the default framing).

* Steps

** Open a dataset's Dependencies tab

Menu: *Data Management > Data Catalogue > Data Librarian*. Select a
dataset with several dependencies and open its view dialog (toolbar
*View Dataset* action, or double-click the row). Switch to the
*Dependencies* tab. Expected: the lineage graph renders and is
initially framed to fit the whole graph in the view (unchanged
baseline behaviour).

*** Result

| Field  | Value |
|--------+-------|
| Status | PASS |

** Mouse wheel zooms in/out centred on the cursor

Hover the mouse over one node in the graph (not the centre of the
view) and scroll the wheel up. Expected: the view zooms in, and the
point under the cursor stays roughly stationary (zoom is anchored
under the mouse, not the view centre). Scroll down. Expected: the view
zooms back out.

*** Result

| Field  | Value |
|--------+-------|
| Status | PASS |

** Click-and-drag pans the view

After zooming in enough that the graph no longer fully fits the
viewport, press and hold the left mouse button over empty canvas and
drag. Expected: the view scrolls/pans smoothly following the drag
(hand-drag scrolling), revealing parts of the graph that were
previously out of view.

*** Result

| Field  | Value |
|--------+-------|
| Status | PASS |

** Reset View restores the original framing

With the view zoomed and panned away from the original framing, click
the *Reset View* button above the graph. Expected: the view returns
exactly to the initial fit-to-window framing seen when the tab was
first opened.

*** Result

| Field  | Value |
|--------+-------|
| Status | PASS |

** Reset View remains correct after switching tabs

Switch to the *Overview* tab and back to *Dependencies*. Zoom/pan
again, then click *Reset View*. Expected: it still resets correctly
(the fit rectangle survives navigating away and back).
