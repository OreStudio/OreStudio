* Goal

The current chart is a QLineSeries of mid ticks; a trading chart shows candlesticks. Aggregate ticks (live and backfilled observations) into OHLC bars per interval and render with QCandlestickSeries (green up / red down). Add a centered faint symbol watermark (e.g. 'EUR/USD'), and an interval selector (1s/5s/15s/1m/5m). Keep the right-side price axis (already moved) and the live latest-value readout. Note the Qt Charts constraint: QCandlestickSeries pairs with a QBarCategoryAxis (categorical X labelled by timestamp), not QDateTimeAxis, so candles are evenly spaced and X-label crowding must be managed (sparse labels / small angled font / capped candle count). Decide whether to replace the line entirely or offer a line/candles toggle.

* Result

Implemented as part of the =FxSpotChartWindow= work (see the chart task).
Ticks and backfilled observations are aggregated into OHLC candles at a
selectable interval (1s/5s/15s/1m/5m); the rightmost candle updates live.
Rendered with =QCandlestickSeries= (emerald up / red down, wicks), a
right-side price axis on a clean pip grid, and a centered symbol watermark.

Decision recorded: rather than replacing the line, the chart offers a
*line/candles toggle* (two toolbar icons) — the two views serve different
uses (continuous trend vs OHLC), and the line view's =QDateTimeAxis= is the
precise-time fallback for the categorical-axis limitation below.

Deferred: candle X labels use a =QBarCategoryAxis= (Qt Charts pairs
candlesticks with a categorical axis), so they are sampled/terse; a custom
non-categorical time axis for candles, hover crosshair, and area-fill remain
follow-ups.
