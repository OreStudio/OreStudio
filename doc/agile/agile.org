* Summary

This project follows an agile process. Work is captured as ideas in
the [[id:d4219199-7b17-4a89-b4bc-6019738642da][product backlog]], promoted into a [[id:699a8d45-b67e-4d3e-9206-24fa17c51ada][story]] when a [[id:0820b7fd-147c-4832-ac25-c043d38d5b61][sprint]] pulls them
in, broken down into [[id:31c868b9-306e-4282-ba8c-07e647021b34][tasks]] that map one-to-one to pull requests, and
grouped into sprints that compose a [[id:ca4d4bde-2eab-4f0a-bc8b-7c0bcb17bc82][version]].

* Detail

This index links to everything under =doc/agile/=:

- *Process* — how the loop runs: opening a sprint, refining the
  backlog, sizing tasks, running retros, closing a sprint.
- *Composition tree* — the active and historical work: every version,
  its sprints, the stories inside them, and the tasks that compose
  those stories. Lives under =doc/agile/versions/=.
- *Product backlog* — ideas not yet pulled into a sprint, split into
  near (next-version candidates) and far (longer-term wishlist).

The /contract/ every document follows (frontmatter, sections, state
machine) lives separately under [[id:b69989f6-19e1-430d-ac6d-b2416e4c433f][doc/meta/]]. In particular,
[[id:8810e0f9-c5b9-4cfa-a710-774ad6c771dd][lifecycle]] describes TODO state transitions generically — it is not
agile-specific. Agile is the loop /around/ those states.
