** domain_name
:PROPERTIES:
:type:     text
:cpp_type: std::string
:nullable: false
:END:

Name of the data domain this subject area belongs to. References
=ores_dq_data_domains_tbl= (soft FK). The real table's natural key is
compound (=name= + =domain_name=); domain_entity's primary key/
exclusion constraint still only support a single column
([[id:52FE40FA-C696-4C77-8A3F-65FB00196EAD][52FE40FA]], BACKLOG), so
=domain_name= is a plain =Column= plus a compound =Indexes= entry
below enforcing the pair's uniqueness for current rows -- weaker than
a true composite primary key/exclusion constraint, but stronger than
the =lookup_entity= version this replaces (which had no compound
uniqueness enforcement at all). Do not regenerate/swap this table
over the hand-written one until 52FE40FA lands.
