* Retrospective

Filled in retrospect, from the v0 record:

- /What worked/ :: the architecture from sprint 02 absorbed the UI-side
  wiring cleanly. The risk module's messaging pattern propagated to
  accounts and sessions without friction.
- /What did not/ :: Windows-specific regressions ate non-trivial time
  again. CTest's single-test-per-suite quirk (and OpenSSL leaks under
  Valgrind) ended the sprint =BLOCKED= rather than fixed; they carry
  over to a later sprint.
- /Carry forward to v2/ :: blocked items become explicit successors
  rather than disappearing into retro bullets.
