* Acceptance

- Document specifies the chosen generation method per asset class (IR, FX, equity, credit, commodity, inflation, each vol surface type, correlation) with a brief rationale.
- Cross-asset consistency constraints enumerated explicitly: for each pair of related data items (FX spot + FX vol surface, IR discount curve + FX forward via CIP, equity spot + equity vol surface, IR curve + swaption vol, credit spread + discount curve, commodity forward + IR discount), the document states the mathematical relationship that must hold and how the generation procedure enforces it — not just that "consistency is important" but the specific constraint equation or calibration dependency.
- Time evolution strategy documented: how the market data environment is advanced through time (e.g. scenario generation frequency, which asset classes move together, how vol surfaces are re-calibrated after a spot move).
- Implementation roadmap: phases, dependencies between asset classes, what can be done independently, and what the minimum viable environment looks like (the smallest consistent subset that prices something interesting).
- Out-of-scope items listed explicitly (e.g. full stochastic vol calibration, real-data replication).
- Filed under doc/knowledge/domain/ as a top-level knowledge doc and linked from the story.
