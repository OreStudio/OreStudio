* Goal

Deliver three bundled documentation improvements to the cybernetics
meta-layer:

1. A standalone *cybernetics summary page* (=doc/meta/cybernetics.org=)
   that explains what cybernetics is and replaces the bare Wikipedia
   link currently used in several docs.
2. Expand [[id:926B916E-A57E-498E-99AB-720B583C0362][Cybernetic Levels]] (=doc/meta/cybernetic_levels.org=) with a
   section on how ORE Studio's approach connects to and diverges from
   Beer's Viable System Model, including known limitations.
3. A new *Viable System Model knowledge page*
   (=doc/meta/viable_system_model.org=) introducing Beer's VSM, linked
   from Cybernetic Levels and the cybernetics summary.

The three pages form a coherent reading path: cybernetics (concept) →
VSM (the model) → Cybernetic Levels (how we apply it in ORE Studio).
