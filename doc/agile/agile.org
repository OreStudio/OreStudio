* Summary

This project follows an agile process: work is captured as ideas in
the /product backlog/, promoted into /stories/ when a sprint pulls
them in, broken down into /tasks/ that map one-to-one to pull
requests, and grouped into /sprints/ that compose a /version/. The
operational artefacts of this process live under [[id:b69989f6-19e1-430d-ac6d-b2416e4c433f][doc/agile/]]; the
contract they all follow lives under [[id:b69989f6-19e1-430d-ac6d-b2416e4c433f][doc/meta/]].

** Process highlights

- /One task, one PR/. Tasks are the smallest unit of work and map
  one-to-one to pull requests; bigger units split until they fit.
- /Sprints are bounded/. Around eighty hours of work per sprint, then
  it closes with a release.
- /Captures preserve identity/. When a capture is promoted into a
  sprint as a story, the file's =:ID:= is preserved so existing links
  keep working — only the =#+type:= flips from =capture= to =story=
  and the file moves into the sprint folder.
- /Folders encode composition, not state/. A document's TODO state
  lives on its =* Status= table; folders never carry state.
