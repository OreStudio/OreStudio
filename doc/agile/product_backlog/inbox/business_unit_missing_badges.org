#+description: Business Unit's status/type-like fields aren't wired to badge_key, so its list and detail views show plain text where other refdata entities show colour-coded badges -- a candidate for the existing badge-adoption coverage audit.

* What

=business_unit='s =status= column (Active/Inactive/Closed, a
hardcoded enum rather than a lookup table) has no =is_badge=/
=badge_key= wiring in =ores.refdata.business_unit.org=, so the list
column and detail combo both render as plain text/string rather than
the colour-coded badge other status-like fields across the codebase
get (e.g. =book_status=-backed entities).

* Why

Found while manually verifying the Org Explorer business unit
edit/history actions after a codegen regen; not a regression from
that change, but a pre-existing gap the badge-adoption coverage audit
task should pick up.
